All about Human being

Humans (Homo sapiens) are the most widespread and dominant species on Earth. We are primates characterized by bipedalism, large brains, and complex social structures. Our ability for abstract thought, language, and tool use sets us apart from other animals. We are capable of creating art, music, and technology. We have spread across the globe, adapting to diverse environments and building complex societies. However, our activities have also significantly impacted the planet, leading to environmental concerns.
